Meatball Nirvana

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 pound extra lean ground beef
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 1/2 cup seasoned bread crumbs
  • 1/3 cup skim milk
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons Italian seasoning
  • 3/4 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 3/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
  • 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic salt
  • 1 dash hot pepper sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C)
  2. Place ground beef, onion, bread crumbs, milk, parmesan cheese, Worcestershire sauce, Italian seasoning, oregano, red pepper flakes, salt, garlic salt, and hot pepper sauce into a large bowl; mix until evenly combined. Shape the mixture into 12 meatballs and place onto a baking shee.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until no longer pink in the center, 20 to 25 minutes.
